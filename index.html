Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? I've got answers. Here's what most people want to know.

Do I need to buy a domain first?

Nope! You can get started without a domain. I'll help you choose and set up the perfect domain name as part of the process, or you can bring your own if you already have one.

How long does it actually take?

Most websites go live within 3-5 business days. Simple sites can be done in 2-3 days, while more complex projects might take up to a week. I'll give you an exact timeline after we chat about your needs.

What if I don't know what to write?

No worries! I'll guide you through what content you need and can help you write it. Many clients start with just basic info about their business, and we build from there. You don't need to be a copywriter.

Can I make changes after the site is done?

Absolutely. I include basic content updates for the first month, and I'm always available for ongoing changes. I'll also show you how to make simple text updates yourself if you want to.
