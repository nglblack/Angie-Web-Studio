The Studio

Angie Web Studio was created to solve a common problem: most local businesses either can't afford a website or end up with one that doesn't reflect their value.

That's where I come in.

I build clean, hand-coded websites that are fast, affordable, and done in days—not weeks. Whether you're a service provider, event organizer, or freelancer, you shouldn't have to overpay for something that simply works and looks good.

No fluff. No subscriptions. Just results.

Meet the Maker

Hi, I'm Angie, a web developer who builds fast, clean websites for people who just need something that works. I hand-code every site myself using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No bloated platforms. No gimmicks. Just lean, scalable code that looks great on any device.

With a background in user experience and a Google UX Design Professional Certificate, I focus on building websites that are both functional and easy to use—for you and your visitors.

Most of my clients are solo business owners or event organizers who need a solid web presence without the tech overwhelm. I'm here to make that simple.
