Common Questions

How long does it take?

Most websites are ready to launch in 2-4 business days once I have your content.

Do I need a domain first?

No. I can help you choose and connect one if needed as an add-on service.

What if I don't know what to write?

I offer copywriting support and a simple guide to help you write content that works.

Is this a monthly subscription?

No. You pay once and the site is yours. No monthly fees unless you request updates.

What if I need changes later?

Post-launch updates start at $25. I'm here to help your site grow with your business.

Do you provide hosting?

Yes, your website will be hosted for free, and I'll help you connect a custom domain. You'll also get professional email forwarding if you'd like. There are no ongoing hosting fees, and you'll fully own your site.
